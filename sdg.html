SDG Goal 10: REDUCE INEQUALITY

Reduced Inequalities aims to reduce inequality within and among countries by 2030. This goal focuses on ensuring equal opportunities, combating social, economic, and political inequalities, and promoting social inclusion, regardless of age, gender, race, or disability. It seeks to create fairer societies where everyone can thrive and benefit from progress and opportunities.
